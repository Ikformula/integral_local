# ECS Agent Guide: Detailed Manual for System Users

This guide is designed for ECS agents and staff who use the ECS (External Client System) platform. It provides step-by-step instructions for all major ECS processes, referencing the system's routes and features. Use this guide to understand your tasks and how to navigate the ECS portal efficiently.

---

## 1. ECS Dashboard
- **Route:** `/ecs-dashboard`
- **Purpose:** View overall ECS status, summaries, and quick links.
- **How to Use:**
  - Access the dashboard for a snapshot of client balances, recent activities, and system alerts.

## 2. ECS Activities Log
- **Route:** `/ecs-activities`
- **Purpose:** Review all ECS-related activities and logs.
- **How to Use:**
  - Use filters to find specific actions or users.
  - Only users with the right permissions can view logs.

## 3. ECS Reports
- **Route:** `/ecs-reports`
- **Purpose:** Generate and view timely ECS reports.
- **How to Use:**
  - Select date ranges and report types.
  - Export or print reports for record-keeping.

## 4. ECS Portal Users Management
- **Routes:** `/ecs_portal_users`
- **Purpose:** Manage users who access the ECS portal.
- **How to Use:**
  - List, add, update, or remove portal users.
  - Ensure users have correct permissions for their roles.

## 5. ECS Clients Management
- **Routes:** `/ecs-clients`
- **Purpose:** Manage ECS client records.
- **How to Use:**
  - View all clients, add new ones, edit details, or remove clients.
  - Access individual client profiles for transaction history and balances.

## 6. ECS Bookings
- **Routes:** `/ecs_bookings`
- **Purpose:** Handle ticket bookings for clients.
- **How to Use:**
  - Create new bookings, select clients, and store booking details.
  - (Advanced) Manage flight transactions and flights via AJAX if enabled.

## 7. ECS Flights (AJAX)
- **Routes:** `/ecs_flights_ajax`
- **Purpose:** Manage flight records using AJAX for faster updates.
- **How to Use:**
  - Add, update, or delete flight records as needed.

## 8. ECS Flight Transactions (AJAX)
- **Routes:** `/ecs_flight_transactions_ajax`
- **Purpose:** Manage flight transaction records using AJAX.
- **How to Use:**
  - Add, update, or delete flight transaction records.

## 9. ECS Reconciliations
- **Routes:** `/ecs_reconciliations`
- **Purpose:** Reconcile client accounts and resolve discrepancies.
- **How to Use:**
  - Create new reconciliations, review, edit, or delete as needed.
  - Use this to ensure client balances are accurate.

## 10. ECS Refunds
- **Routes:** `/ecs_refunds`
- **Purpose:** Process ticket refunds for clients.
- **How to Use:**
  - Create individual or group refunds.
  - Store refund records and review details.

## 11. ECS Client Account Summaries
- **Routes:** `/ecs_client_account_summaries`
- **Purpose:** View and manage summaries of client account balances.
- **How to Use:**
  - Create new summaries, review client balances, and access historical data.

---

## General Agent Workflow
1. **Login to ECS Portal** with your credentials.
2. **Check Dashboard** for updates and alerts.
3. **Review Activities Log** if you need to audit actions or track changes.
4. **Manage Clients and Bookings:**
   - Add new clients or update existing ones.
   - Create bookings and process transactions.
5. **Handle Refunds and Reconciliations:**
   - Process refunds as needed.
   - Reconcile accounts to fix any discrepancies.
6. **Generate Reports:**
   - Use the reports section to view summaries and export data.
7. **Manage Portal Users:**
   - Ensure only authorized users have access.

---

## Permissions & Access
- Some routes and actions require special permissions (e.g., managing processes, viewing logs, supervising agents).
- If you cannot access a feature, contact your supervisor or ECS administrator.

---

## Tips for Agents
- Always double-check client details before processing transactions or refunds.
- Use filters and search tools to quickly find records.
- Export reports regularly for backup and compliance.
- If you encounter errors or duplicates, report them to support.

---

## Need Help?
- Refer to this manual for guidance.
- For technical issues, contact ECS support or your supervisor.
